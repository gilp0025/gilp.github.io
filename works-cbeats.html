The goal

CB Eats is an app focused on ordering food online from Cape Breton Restaurant areas in just a few taps. Unfortunately, there wasn't many visual updates since its launch and its navigation and key elements are obsolete. The goal was to give the app a new facelift, increase its visual appeal, and enhance the user experience through UI, making easier to find your next meal.



The Design process

The process was divided in 4 phases:
1. Understand: the requirements, create user personas and define use cases.
2. Research: Analyze competitors, research latest UX trends and keep an eye on guidelines.
3. Understand: gather ideas, draw skeches & wireframes, and evaluate and re-draw.
4. Design: design images, create prototypes, and define UX guidelines.

User research

Project goals:
1. To help people in Cape Breton reach out to various restaurants of their choices.
2. To help restaurants reach out to a wider market.
3. To deliver orders to respective customers who place orders using the app.
4. o improve the cashless economy system.

User goals:
1. See various dishes prepared by various restaurants in Cape Breton.
2. Place orders.
3. Request to deliver o pick up the orders.
4. Track order.
5. Pay through the app.

Target users:
1. People who's busy and want a different kind of food.
2. People who don't know how to cook and wants a good meal.
3. People hanging together who wants to provide food for the group.
4. Worink people who doen't had time to prepare a meal.
