Hi there, I'm Marilia!

I'm currently a student at Algonquin College doing the Interactive Media Program and a former graphic designer.

Always inspired by designs that break down complex problems into simple intuitive solutions. In this sometimes chaotic world, I strive to design solutions with simplicity in mind. My natural curiosity always propels me to be on the search for better ways of doing things and solving problems to help make peoples’ lives a little easier, one design problem at a time.

My experience in graphic design and advertising taught me a lot about the importance of fostering a collaborative environment for more innovative solutions. Along the way, I also learned to balance solutions between customers' needs and business goals.

In my off-time I enjoy camping, exploring new neighborhoods, doing calligraphy and reading good books; as you never know where the next design inspiration will strike.
