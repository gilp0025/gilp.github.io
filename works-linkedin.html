What is "Job Seeker"?

Linkedin's Job Seeker is a paid subscription tool that gives to the user more emphasis in the search for new job positions, making the profile stand up. This feature puts the user in contact with headhunters, hiring managers and business companies who are searching for new employees, making easier to establish new connections and providing insights in how to enhance your profile to appear more attractive to the job market.



The brief

Create an ad showing, in a simple way, why the user should contract the Job Seeker feature and how this can lead the user to its desire position



The idea

The mote was focused on why it's important to make meaningful connections.
As Linkedin it's a free social media, anyone can create a profile and start to connect to people, but this doesn't mean you are doing the right connections our appearing on a job search. For this, we choose to illustrate our ad with famous pop culture figures, showing that even though this person could have the right amount of fame and be in touch with many people, without the right connection it's effort could fail.



The Design process

The process was divided in 4 phases:
1. Understand: the tool's requirements, figure out easy and visual ways to translate the ideia.
2. Research: Analyze possible paths, research the best way to translate the ideia.
3. Understand: gather ideas, draw sketches, try titles, and evaluate and re-draw.
4. Design: design how to approach in the simplest way possible making clear the concept is understandable to different people with different backgrounds.
